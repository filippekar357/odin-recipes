Description

It's easy to make this thin crust pizza dough recipe with a few ingredients you may likely already have on hand.

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Flour
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Activate the yeast with sugar and water
  2. Make the dough
  3. Knead the dough
  4. Roll the dough into a circle
